# Scrumlo — Terms (stub)

Scrumlo is a free, open-source, no-login tool for running sprint ceremonies over a shared link. It is intended for **trusted shared links among colleagues**, not public boards.

- Rooms are ephemeral: room state is held by a Cloudflare Durable Object and deleted when everyone leaves / the room idles out. There is no database and no account system.
- Do not use Scrumlo to share unlawful content or to harass. Room names are filtered; a room can be reported and killed.
