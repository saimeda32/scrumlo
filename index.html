Scrumlo — estimate, retro, forgotten

Free, open-source sprint ceremonies in one shareable link: blind-reveal planning poker, facilitated retrospectives on a zoomable canvas, roadmap boards, team polls with live word clouds, team health checks, and a random picker wheel. No login, no database — nothing is kept after the room ends.

  • Planning poker · blind simultaneous reveal, custom decks, story backlog with per-story decisions
  • Sprint retrospective · 14 formats, blind brainstorm, drag-to-cluster, dot-voting, action items
  • Polls & word clouds · hide results until reveal, question queue, save the cloud as an image
  • Health check, roadmap board, random picker, shared timer, Markdown/PNG/PDF export
