The facilitated flow

  1. Brainstorm (blind) — everyone adds stickies; other people's notes stay hidden server-side until the reveal, so ideas are independent.
  2. Group — drag stickies onto each other to cluster themes; votes roll up automatically.
  3. Vote — dot-vote the themes that deserve discussion time.
  4. Discuss — spotlight cards one by one, mark them discussed, capture action items with owners.

What you get

  • 14 retro formats — Start/Stop/Continue, Sailboat, Starfish, Mad/Sad/Glad, 4Ls and more.
  • Anonymous mode — when the topic is touchy, names come off the stickies in every phase.
  • Live cursors, reactions, spotlight — it feels like one shared board, because it is.
  • Action items with owners — flagged on the board and listed first in the export.
  • Export — Markdown summary or a full-board PNG/PDF, generated in your browser.

FAQ

Is the retro tool free?

Yes, entirely — no seat limits, no premium formats. And it's open-source.

Can teammates see my stickies while I write?

Not during blind brainstorm — the server withholds everyone's notes until the facilitator reveals. Independent ideas first, discussion second.

Can we run it anonymously?

Yes — the facilitator can switch the retro to anonymous and author names disappear from stickies in every phase, including discussion.

How do we keep the outcomes?

One click exports a Markdown summary (action items first) or a PNG/PDF of the whole board — before the room forgets everything.

What size team does it handle?

Rooms are tested with 20+ people on the board at once, cursors and all.
